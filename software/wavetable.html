WaveTable

WaveTable is a sort of waveform editor / looper for interactive tabletops, developed together with Anna Xambó during our Master at UPF. We attended a course by Sergi Jordà and Martin Kaltenbruner, who were working on the reacTable along with Günter Geiger and Marcos Alonso. We used the same technology to explore waveform manipulation using tangible interfaces.
